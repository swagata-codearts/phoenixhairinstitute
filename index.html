Welcome to Phonex Hair Institute

NuHair Transplants offers state of the art surgical and non-surgical hair restoration for treatment of both male and female pattern baldness and hair loss, or otherwise known as androgenic alopecia.

Nuhair Transplants has clinic locations in Vancouver, Edmonton, Winnipeg, Calgary, Saskatoon, Surrey, Kamloops, Victoria, Pitt Meadows and Fort McMurray.

Our international team of hair transplant specialists have many decades of combined experience, each has been selected and assembled from across North America specifically for their outstanding accomplishments in the field of hair transplantation.
